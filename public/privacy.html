Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Scorpio Zodiac ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash)
  • Birth date — optionally provided to generate your personalized horoscope
  • Usage data — pages visited and features used, collected anonymously

How We Use Your Data

  • To generate your daily personalized horoscope based on your birth date
  • To manage your account and authenticate you securely
  • To send password reset emails if requested
  • To improve the quality of our content and features

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not send unsolicited marketing emails
  • We do not store payment information (we have no paid features)

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database hosted in the EU). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. Authentication tokens are stored as secure HTTP-only cookies.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Proprietary content engine — our horoscope generation system
  • Railway — server hosting
  • Google — optional Google sign-in

Cookies

We use a single session cookie (sz_token) to keep you logged in. This cookie is HTTP-only and expires after 7 days. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Your Rights

  • Access — you can view your stored data in your profile
  • Correction — you can update your name, email, and birth date at any time
  • Deletion — you can request full deletion of your account and data by contacting us

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at our contact page.

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
